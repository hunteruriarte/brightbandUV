The problem

Most people don't know when they've had too much sun.

UV radiation is invisible. By the time your skin starts to burn, the damage is already done. Existing solutions like sunscreen reminders don't account for actual UV levels, your skin type, or cumulative daily exposure.

Bright Band changes that by putting real-time UV data on your wrist, with an alert system that tells you exactly when to seek shade or reapply protection.

Features

Everything you need to protect yourself.

UV photodiode sensor

Upward-facing sensor orientation minimizes cosine error for accurate UV index readings throughout the day.

Instant LED alert

Lights up the moment your UV exposure crosses a dangerous threshold so you can act immediately.

All-day battery

Tested at 8+ hours of continuous operation — enough for a full day outdoors on a single charge.

Wristband design

Lightweight and comfortable. Built to wear all day without getting in the way of your activities.

Onboard microcontroller

Processes sensor readings locally in real time with no phone or app required.

Affordable by design

Engineered from the ground up to be accessible — not a luxury gadget, a practical everyday tool.

UV index explained

Know your UV risk level.

The UV index measures the intensity of ultraviolet radiation from the sun. Bright Band monitors this in real time and alerts you when you reach dangerous levels.

Low (0–2)Safe
Moderate (3–5)Use caution
High (6–7)Bright Band alerts
Very high (8–10)Seek shade
Extreme (11+)Stay indoors
How it works

Simple. Wearable. Always on.

1

Put it on

Strap Bright Band to your wrist like any other wearable. It powers on automatically.

2

It monitors

The UV sensor continuously reads radiation levels and feeds data to the onboard microcontroller.

3

You get alerted

When UV exposure hits a harmful level, the LED alert fires immediately so you know to act.

4

Stay protected

Seek shade, reapply sunscreen, or cover up — and Bright Band keeps monitoring the rest of the day.
